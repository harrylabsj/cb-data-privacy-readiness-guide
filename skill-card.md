## Description: <br>
Get a practical, step-by-step privacy compliance checklist for any market, including GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, and PIPL, with actionable readiness planning and templates for privacy policies, cookie banners, data-processing agreements, and DSAR workflows. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders, product managers, operations teams, marketers, and compliance coordinators use this skill to prepare cross-border privacy readiness plans for overseas digital businesses handling customer data. It produces structured checklists, matrices, templates, decision criteria, assumptions, and validation questions for qualified privacy counsel. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill discusses privacy and data-transfer readiness, which can be mistaken for jurisdiction-specific legal advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as planning support only and use qualified privacy counsel for final compliance decisions. <br>
Risk: Prompts may include sensitive customer, vendor, or operational details while building data inventories and privacy workflows. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering unnecessary sensitive details and minimize or anonymize customer and vendor information in prompts. <br>
Risk: Marketplace capability tags indicate financial authority and purchasing ability even though the skill evidence describes no such behavior. <br>
Mitigation: Rely on the security summary that the skill has no executable behavior or external actions, and correct marketplace metadata before operational use. <br>
